Contact Us

Celestial Church of Christ Ebenezery Parish is Lagos West Central Province "F" Headquarters. We're glad you could reach us via this platform. We worship in holiness and reverence, also preaches the good news of the messiah - Jesus Christ and the imminent Kingdom of God through evangelism, Bible Classes, Bible Lectures, and rendition of church hymnals. Join us on any of our service and witness the presence of the Holy Spirit.

If you are intrested in fellowshipping with us or joining the Church Baptism and other ministerial group within the church, or in need of Pastorial Counseling, Please contact us via Our social media Platform or visit our church at 15/17, Tinuade Oyeniran Street, Old Akesan Road, Egan, Lagos State, Nigeria.
