Choir

CCC EBENEZERY PARISH CHOIR

C.C.C Ebenezery Parish Choir is an holy spirit filled choir of C.C.C Ebenezery Parish under the oneness of the Celestial Church of Christ body with spiritually inspired music of praise-worship to the most High, Our Lord Jesus Christ. Join us on any of our service and witness the presence of the Holy Spirit.

And the ransomed of the Lord shall return with songs and everlasting Joy
"AnD those the Lord has rescued will return. They will enter Zion with singing; everlasting joy will crown their heads. Gladness and Joy will overtake them, and sorrow and sighing will flee away."

Isaiah 35:10 NIV

Our Mission.

The aim of the choir is to win souls for christ by ministering to people through our songs and music about the word of God.

Choir Album - Confidence(Igboya)

In 2017, the choir produced an "Album" titled "Confidence[Igboya]". It is a Six Track album with each track titled "Ninu Igboya", "Celestial yi dun", "Prayer for Nigeria", "Ogo", "Bamise Oluwa", "Motimo" respectively.

2. Celestial yi dun
