Welcome to Ebenezery Parish

Celestial Church of Christ Ebenezery Parish is Lagos West Central Province "F" Headquarters. We're glad you could reach us via this platform. It's a Parish where we worship in holiness and reverence, also preaches the good news of the messiah - Jesus Christ and the imminent Kingdom of God through evangelism, Bible Classes, Bible Lectures, and rendition of church hymnals. Join us on any of our service and witness the presence of the Holy Spirit.

Volunteer With Us.

As Christians, we are tasked with utilizing these God's given gifts and talent in our community and within our local church. Using your skills as a volunteer is a great way to serve the church while simultaneously lightening the load of a church’s staff. Volunteering has a spiritual benefit to us as well. At the same time, they serve not only as a helping hand, but also as a witness of the Gospel message within the community.
