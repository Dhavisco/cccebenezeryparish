Join the Team

God made us for His purposes way before we ever realized it. Ephesians 2:10 says, “We are His workmanship, created in Christ Jesus for good works, which God prepared beforehand, that we should walk in them.” Colossians 3:23-24 says, “Whatever you do, work heartily, as for the Lord and not for men, knowing that from the Lord you will receive an inheritance as your reward. You are serving the Lord Christ.” We serve and volunteer confidently, knowing that God has prepared the way for us!

The purpose of service is so God can reach others through our hands. In II Cor. 9:12-13, the Bible says that the service we provide not only meets needs but expresses thanks to God and leads others to praise God.


Volunteer With Us Through the Following Ministries

Sidemen/Sidewoman & Ushering.

The Ushering department is involved in every activity of the church. Organisation and Humility is required. You can Join the Sidemen and Sidewomen team.

Kids Church.

Do you know how to communicate, care and relate with kids? Can you teach and preach the word of God? You can Join The Sunday School department to help raise our kids in the right way and manner of Christ.

Music Team/Choir

The Choir department consist of a group of talented singers, instrumentalist and music directors. Also, individuals that support the choir financially, socially, technically. The choir won the Zone F Youth Organisation musical competition 2017 tagged "Let It Shine". The choir also won the Celestial Avenue musical competition 2018 tagged "Heavenly Praise on Earth". Furthermore, in september 2018 and July 2019,the choir took a 2nd position in the musical competition of Zone F Youth Organisation and Celestial Avenue respectively. In 2017, the choir produced an "Album" titled "Confidence[Igboya]" . The aim of the choir is to win souls for christ by ministering to people through our songs and music about the word of God.
If you fit into any of these or you are interested in using your talent to the glory of God, you can Join the Choir.

Prayer Team.

The purpose of the Prayer Ministry Team is to facilitate the move of the Holy Spirit in individuals in church and in personal interactions. The purpose is to release God's love and power toward those who indicate a desire for prayer and to bless, strengthen, encourage, and comfort. Prayer team serve people through prayer, trained and authorized to minister directly to people in corporate gatherings and maybe sent out to minister. Where two or more believers are gathered, God is with them. When christians come together and have ministry through prayer, there are honoring God and casting their cares upon Him. God dwells in those who believe on him, and his presence is always with you. You can join the Prayer Team

Administrative Support.

The church administrative support team attends to the many different office and administrative duties of the church. Church administrative support team serve beneath the church leaders and pastor, attending to the general running of the church office and addressing the church’s short and long term business dealings. If you have these skills and interested, you can Join the team.

Production Design.

The Production Design team helps in planning church activities. They also make sure everything needed during the church service is available and in good condition. They inform the church about various necessities and if anything is wrong or bad it is brought to the attention of the church. You can join the team if interested.

Technical Team.

The Technical Department exist for this purpose: to invest ourselves in a ministry that will directly support and reinforce the presentation of biblical truths that requires the support of lighting, audio, video, sound or any other form of technical support during church services, events as well as stage support. We will strive to do this in a manner that is honouring to our God. You can join the technical department.

Communications & PR

The Communication and Public Relation team manage and effectively deliver vital information to the church community while also leaving room to be creative and forge communication paths. The Team also handles the social media platforms, website, designs for programs of the church. If you have any skill related to this field like content creation, web development, social media, blogs, and graphics design, you can join the team.
